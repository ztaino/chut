# Privacy Policy for Chut

_Last updated: April 24, 2026_

Chut ("the app") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- No accounts, no sign-in.
- No analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs.
- No network requests involving user data.
- Game preferences (language, difficulty) are stored only on your device using Apple's standard local storage (`UserDefaults` / `@AppStorage`).

Chut is designed for offline play. The app does not contact any external server.

## Children's Privacy

Chut does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
